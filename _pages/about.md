Alexander von Humboldt Research Fellow at the [Chair of Digital Water Systems, TU Berlin](https://www.tu.berlin/en/), specializing in the design, algorithmic optimization, and resilient management of urban water distribution systems (WDS) under uncertainty. My work integrates algorithmic fairness, socio-economic equity, and contaminant transport kinetics into water infrastructure modeling frameworks.

I serve as an **Associate Editor** for ASCE's *Journal of Water Resources Planning and Management* and as **Chair of the Task Committee on Generative AI in Publishing** (ASCE-EWRI).

**Research Interests:** Optimization and control of water distribution systems · Water quality and resilience modeling under uncertainty · Equity and fairness in human-water infrastructure systems · Contaminant transport kinetics · Data-driven infrastructure management
